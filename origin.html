ORIGIN STORY

Through the years, Shean has collected over $4,000 in shoes and always looking for sneakers to add to his collection. His love of sneakers was inspired by his uncle and his dad who also had a passion for the shoe game. Shean's uncle, Eric Juta, owns more than 150 pairs of shoes putting his collection at more than $15,000. With the every growing design industry, retro designed sneakers and new collaborations and colorways are destined to releases in the future for all sneakerheads, new and old, to collect.
